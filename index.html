BIO

Taekkyung Oh is a Ph.D student in System Security Lab at KAIST, advised by Professor Yongdae Kim. He received a Master degree from Graduate School of Information Security at KAIST. His research interests are Cellular Network Security, Wireless System Security, Cyber-Physical System (CPS) Security, and Usable Security.
